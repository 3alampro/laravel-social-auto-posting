Networks where Larasap can auto-posting

  • Auto-post to your account.
  • Ability to attach photo to tweets.
  • Auto-post to your page.
  • Ability to send text, photo, and link.
  • Auto-post to your Telegram channel.
  • Ability to send text, photo, audio, document, video, voice, group if photo or video as a album, location, venue, and contact.
  • Ability to send message with url inline keyboard.
